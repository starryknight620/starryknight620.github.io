About

Hello! My name is Naveen Gupta. I'm a NYC native and have been here for 22 years of my life. The other eight were in Boston, where I attended Boston University for the first four and worked at IBM followed by Akamai for the latter four.
I'm currently employed at Akamai Technologies as a product manager, working on the company's Services and Support product offerings. Prior to this, I was a technical support engineer for 6 years at Akamai. After many years of various support roles, I decided to pivot to a product management role which oversees the inner workings of our services and support teams, and offer my expertise in the domain for product improvements.
In addition, I'm an investor in Kolkata Chai Co, located in NYC's vibrant East Village. As an investor, I have a hands on approach to things and work part time at the cafe on Saturdays. A portion of my cafe wages every summer are donated to various organizations every summer.
Outside of Akamai Technologies and Kolkata Chai Co, my interests are cooking , photography , swimming, and dancing. I became more involved with dance as I took various dance based fitness classes such as Zumba, BollyX, and Masala Bhangra .
